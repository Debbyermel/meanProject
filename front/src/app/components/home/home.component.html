What is Irongram?

IronGram is a web app for Ironhacker students to show case their profile. The application and service allows users to share publicly links of their demos projects that was developed during the bootcamp. Also It allows IronHackers to keep in touch with each other throuth our private blog.

How it Works?

As an ironhacker you can sign-in and customise your profile. After it is done your profile is available publicly. Don't forget that you can keep in touch with the Ironhack community.

What if I'm not an Ironhacker?

You are more than welcome to browser the IronHackers profiles and check their projects. Also you can connect by social media and email with them.
Hopefuly you get inspired and soon you will be part of IronGram too.
